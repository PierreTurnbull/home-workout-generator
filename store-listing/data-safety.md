# Play Console data safety answers

Use these answers when completing the Data safety form for Home Workout Generator.

## Overview

- **Does your app collect or share any of the required user data types?** No
- **Is all of the user data collected by your app encrypted in transit?** Not applicable
- **Do you provide a way for users to request that their data is deleted?** Yes

## Data types

No data is collected, shared, or transmitted off-device.

Workout history, circuit settings, and language preference are stored locally in the app using on-device storage only.

## Data deletion

Users can delete individual past workouts in the History screen. Uninstalling the app removes all local data.

## Account creation

The app does not require account creation.

## Third-party links

The exercise guide may open an external image search link in the browser. That action is user-initiated and optional.

https://pierreturnbull.github.io/home-workout-generator/privacy-policy.html

Use the email tied to your Google Play developer account, or a support address you control.
